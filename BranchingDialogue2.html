Branching Dialogue

I wanted to practice writing and editing branching dialogue. I spent some of my free time writing up branching dialogue as you might see in a video game. The boxes are not complete lines of dialogue, but just the conceptual pieces of what the dialogue would reveal or what it would result in. Some of my work is shown below.
